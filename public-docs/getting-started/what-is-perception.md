# What is Perception

> A comprehensive Bitcoin market intelligence platform that tracks sentiment, media coverage, and emerging trends across the crypto ecosystem.

## Overview

Perception aggregates and analyzes Bitcoin-related content from across the media landscape. Instead of manually monitoring dozens of news sources, social feeds, and industry publications, you get a single dashboard with everything you need.

## Data Coverage

Perception monitors:

- **Mainstream News**: Reuters, Bloomberg, Financial Times, Forbes, CNBC
- **Crypto Publications**: CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, The Block, Decrypt
- **Social Platforms**: X/Twitter, Reddit discussions
- **Industry Sources**: Company announcements, regulatory filings

All data is processed, enriched with sentiment analysis, and made searchable within minutes of publication.

---

## How It Works

1. **Data Collection**: Our automated systems continuously gather content from 50+ sources
2. **Processing**: Each piece of content is analyzed for sentiment, topics, and relevance
3. **Trend Detection**: Our proprietary systems identify patterns and emerging narratives hourly
4. **Delivery**: Access insights through the dashboard, alerts, or scheduled reports
