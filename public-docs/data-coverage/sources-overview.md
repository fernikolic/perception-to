# Data Sources Overview

> What Perception monitors and how

## Overview

Perception aggregates Bitcoin and cryptocurrency coverage from 50+ media sources, processing 560,000+ data points to provide comprehensive market intelligence. Our data spans from 2011 to present, with new content added continuously.

---

## Source Categories

### Mainstream Media

Major news outlets that cover Bitcoin as part of broader financial and technology coverage.

**Examples:**
- Reuters
- Bloomberg
- Financial Times
- Forbes
- CNBC
- BBC
- The Wall Street Journal
- The New York Times

**Characteristics:**
- Broad audience reach
- Generally neutral/factual tone
- Less frequent Bitcoin coverage
- High authority and credibility

### Crypto-Native Publications

Media outlets dedicated to cryptocurrency and blockchain coverage.

**Examples:**
- CoinDesk
- Cointelegraph
- The Block
- Decrypt
- Bitcoin Magazine
- CryptoSlate
- Crypto News

**Characteristics:**
- Deep industry expertise
- Frequent updates
- Technical depth
- Industry insider perspective

### Financial Media

Business and investment-focused publications.

**Examples:**
- MarketWatch
- Seeking Alpha
- Yahoo Finance
- Barron's
- Investopedia
- Business Insider

**Characteristics:**
- Investment angle focus
- Market analysis depth
- Price and trading emphasis
- Investor audience

### Social & Community

Social platforms and community discussions.

**Examples:**
- X/Twitter
- Reddit discussions
- Community forums

**Characteristics:**
- Real-time sentiment
- Retail investor perspective
- High volume, variable quality
- Early signal potential

## Data Quality

### What We Process

Every data point includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Headline or post title |
| **Content** | Full text when available |
| **Date** | Publication timestamp |
| **Source** | Media outlet or platform |
| **Sentiment** | AI-classified (Positive/Neutral/Negative) |
| **Topics** | Extracted themes and categories |

### Quality Measures

- **Deduplication**: Same content from multiple aggregators is merged
- **Validation**: Content is verified for relevance
- **Sentiment Accuracy**: AI classification with human-trained models
- **Timeliness**: Most content indexed within minutes of publication

---

## Geographic Coverage

Perception monitors sources from around the world:

- **North America**: US and Canadian publications
- **Europe**: UK, EU, and regional sources
- **Asia**: Major Asian financial and crypto media
- **Global**: Wire services and international publications

---

## What We Don't Track

To maintain quality and relevance:

- Spam or promotional content
- Non-English sources (primarily)
- Personal blogs without editorial standards
- Paywalled content without public excerpts
- Duplicate content farms
