## Overview

The Intelligence Feed is your central hub for monitoring Bitcoin-related media coverage. It aggregates content from 50+ sources into a single, searchable feed that updates continuously.

---

## What You'll See

Each item in the feed includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | The headline or title of the content |
| **Outlet** | Which publication or source it came from |
| **Date** | When it was published |
| **Sentiment** | AI-classified as Positive, Neutral, or Negative |
| **Content Preview** | A snippet of the full content |

---

## How Data Is Processed

1. **Collection**: New content is gathered continuously from all monitored sources
2. **Analysis**: Each piece is processed for sentiment, topics, and relevance
3. **Enrichment**: Additional metadata is added (outlet category, author info when available)
4. **Indexing**: Content becomes searchable within minutes of publication

---

## Update Frequency

- **News sources**: Synced multiple times daily
- **Social sources**: Near real-time monitoring
- **Historical data**: Available back to 2011

---

## Tips for Using the Feed

1. **Start with your watchlist**: The feed automatically prioritizes content matching your tracked topics
2. **Use date filters**: Recent coverage is often most actionable
3. **Check sentiment balance**: A sudden shift in sentiment distribution can signal important changes
4. **Save searches**: Store frequently used search queries for quick access
