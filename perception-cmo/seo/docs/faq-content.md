## Homepage FAQ

### Q: What is Perception?
Perception is a crypto media intelligence platform that monitors Bitcoin and cryptocurrency coverage across 250+ sources. It helps PR teams, research analysts, and content creators track narratives, analyze sentiment, and generate intelligence reports with full citations. Think of it as your research assistant for the crypto media landscape.

### Q: How is Perception different from LunarCrush or Santiment?
While LunarCrush and Santiment focus primarily on social media sentiment and on-chain data, Perception specializes in media intelligence across 250+ sources including legacy media (CNBC, Bloomberg, NYT), crypto-native outlets (CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, The Block), and social platforms. Perception is built for PR and communications workflows, not trading.

### Q: What sources does Perception monitor?
Perception monitors 250+ sources including: traditional financial media (CNBC, Bloomberg, Reuters, Wall Street Journal), crypto-native publications (CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, The Block, Decrypt), social platforms (Twitter/X, Reddit), research reports, regulatory announcements, and industry newsletters. We aggregate across the entire crypto media landscape.

### Q: Who is Perception built for?
Perception is built for crypto industry professionals including: PR and communications teams who monitor coverage and track journalists, research analysts who need comprehensive market intelligence, business development teams tracking opportunities, and content creators who need story angles and trend validation.

### Q: How much does Perception cost?
Perception is currently in beta at $99/month with full access to all features. This includes monitoring of 250+ sources, sentiment tracking, narrative analysis, intelligence report generation, and unlimited usage of Spaces and Recipes. No hidden fees or usage limits.

### Q: Does Perception offer a free trial?
Yes, Perception offers a free trial so you can experience the full platform before committing. You get access to all features during the trial period. Sign up at perception.to to get started.

### Q: What are Perception Recipes?
Recipes are AI-powered templates that generate specific deliverables like sector deep dives, executive briefings, PR pitch intelligence, and competitive analysis reports. Each Recipe pulls from your monitored sources and produces citation-backed intelligence in minutes instead of hours.

### Q: What are Perception Spaces?
Spaces let you organize your research by saving articles, tracking specific entities (companies, people, topics), and building personalized intelligence feeds. Think of them as smart folders for your crypto media research that automatically update with relevant coverage.

## Features Page FAQ

### Q: How does Perception's sentiment analysis work?
Perception analyzes coverage across 250+ sources to determine overall sentiment around topics, companies, and narratives. Unlike social-only tools, we include sentiment from professional journalists and publications, giving you a more complete picture of how the media landscape perceives any crypto topic.

### Q: Can I track specific companies or people?
Yes, Spaces allow you to create watchlists for any entity—companies, individuals, tokens, protocols, or topics. You'll get aggregated coverage, sentiment trends, and alerts when significant coverage appears.

### Q: How often is data updated?
Most sources are monitored continuously throughout the day. You can set up email alerts for immediate notification when specific topics or entities receive coverage, or receive daily/weekly digest emails.

### Q: Can I generate reports automatically?
Yes, Recipes let you generate intelligence reports automatically. Set up a Recipe once, and you can run it anytime to get an updated report with the latest coverage and citations. Popular Recipes include sector deep dives, competitive analysis, and executive briefings.

### Q: Does Perception have an API?
API access is coming soon. Join our waitlist to be notified when it launches. In the meantime, you can export data and reports from the platform.

### Q: How do citations work?
Every insight and claim in Perception links back to the original source. When you generate a report, each data point includes a citation you can verify. This ensures your intelligence is backed by evidence, not speculation.

## Use Case: PR Teams FAQ

### Q: Can I track specific journalists?
Yes, you can monitor individual journalists and publications to understand their coverage patterns, sentiment toward topics, and recent articles. This helps you identify the right contacts for pitches and understand their beat.

### Q: Does Perception help with newsjacking?
Absolutely. By monitoring narratives as they develop, you can identify newsjacking opportunities early—when a topic is gaining momentum but before it's saturated. Our trend detection helps you spot windows for timely commentary.

### Q: Can I share reports with clients?
Yes, all reports generated through Recipes can be exported and shared. They're designed to be client-ready with professional formatting and full citations. Many agencies use Perception to deliver regular intelligence reports to clients.

### Q: How does Perception compare to Meltwater or Cision?
Traditional media monitoring tools like Meltwater and Cision aren't built for crypto. They miss crypto-native publications and don't understand the nuances of the space. Perception is purpose-built for crypto media intelligence with specialized source coverage and analysis.
