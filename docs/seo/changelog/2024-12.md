#### Critical Issues Identified
1. **Fear & Greed page invisible** - 851 impressions, 0 clicks, position 68
2. **Market Sentiment underperforming** - 1,106 impressions, 5 clicks, position 27
3. **Zero clicks on most keywords** - Despite ranking positions 5-10
4. **100% non-brand traffic** - No brand awareness established

#### Root Cause
SSR issues confirmed by GSC data. Google indexes pages but content isn't properly rendered, resulting in poor rankings despite impressions.
