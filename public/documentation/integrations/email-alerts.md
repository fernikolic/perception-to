# Email Integration

> Receive intelligence directly in your inbox

## Overview

Perception delivers intelligence via email so you can stay informed without logging into the platform. Configure real-time alerts for breaking news or scheduled digests for comprehensive summaries.

## Email Types

### Real-Time Alerts

Immediate notifications when significant coverage matches your watchlist.

**Sent when:**
- Breaking news about your topics
- High-impact coverage from major outlets
- Regulatory updates for tracked keywords
- Significant sentiment shifts

**Format:**
- Subject: `[Alert Type] Topic: Headline preview...`
- Body: Article details, summary, sentiment, and links

**Limits:**
- Maximum 5 alerts per day
- 2-hour minimum gap between same-keyword alerts

### Weekly Digest

Comprehensive weekly summary of all watchlist activity.

**Includes:**
- Mention counts for each tracked topic
- Week-over-week comparison
- Sentiment breakdown
- Top 5 outlets per topic
- Automatically generated professional analysis

**Delivery:**
- Your chosen day of week
- Your chosen time
- Your local timezone

### Available Options

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Real-time alerts** | On/Off |
| **Alert threshold** | All, Medium, High |
| **Weekly digest** | On/Off |
| **Delivery day** | Sunday - Saturday |
| **Delivery time** | 12am - 11pm |
| **Timezone** | 70+ worldwide options |

## Best Practices

### For Executives
- Enable weekly digest only
- Set to Monday morning for weekly planning
- Use "High" threshold if enabling real-time alerts

### For PR Teams
- Enable both real-time and weekly
- Set real-time threshold to "All" during campaigns
- Review digest every Monday

### For Analysts
- Enable weekly digest for baseline tracking
- Use real-time selectively for critical topics
- Archive digests for trend analysis

## Email Deliverability

### Ensuring Delivery

1. Add `alerts@perception.to` to your contacts
2. Check spam/junk folder initially
3. Whitelist the domain in your email settings
4. Verify your email address is correct in Settings

### Not Receiving Emails?

1. Check notification settings are enabled
2. Verify you have watchlist items configured
3. Confirm email address is correct
4. Check spam folder
5. Contact support if issues persist

---

## Managing Preferences

### From Email

Each email includes links to:
- Manage preferences (opens Settings)
- Pause alerts
- Unsubscribe from specific types

### From App

Go to **Settings > Notifications** to:
- Enable/disable each email type
- Adjust delivery schedule
- Change timezone
- View past reports
