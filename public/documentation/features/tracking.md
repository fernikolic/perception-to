## Overview

Perception's tracking features help you stay on top of the topics, companies, and themes you care about. Create a watchlist, save searches, and let the platform surface relevant coverage automatically.

---

## Watchlist

Your watchlist is a collection of topics you want to monitor continuously.

### Adding a Watchlist Item

1. Navigate to the **Intelligence** page
2. Click **Add to Watchlist**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name**: A friendly label (e.g., "MicroStrategy")
   - **Search Terms**: Keywords to match, separated by commas
   - **Topics** (optional): Categories to associate
   - **Goals** (optional): Notes about why you're tracking this

### Search Term Tips

Be specific to get relevant results:

| Goal | Better Search Terms | Avoid |
|------|---------------------|-------|
| Track a company | "MicroStrategy, MSTR, Saylor Bitcoin" | "Bitcoin company" |
| Monitor a topic | "Bitcoin ETF SEC approval" | "ETF" |
| Follow a person | "Michael Saylor, @saylor" | "CEO" |
| Watch regulation | "Bitcoin regulation SEC, crypto policy" | "regulation" |

### Managing Your Watchlist

- **Edit**: Update search terms as topics evolve
- **Delete**: Remove items you no longer need to track
- **Reorder**: Prioritize your most important items

## Saved Searches

Save search queries you use frequently for quick access.

### How It Works

1. Enter a search in the keyword search field
2. Click the bookmark icon to save
3. Access saved searches from the dropdown menu
4. Click any saved search to run it instantly

### Features

- **Up to 20 saved searches** per account
- **Color coding**: Each search gets a unique color for easy identification
- **Usage tracking**: See when you last used each search
- **Quick access**: Dropdown menu in the search bar

### Managing Saved Searches

- **Remove individual searches**: Click the X on any saved search
- **Clear all**: Use the menu option to remove all saved searches

## Search Capabilities

### What You Can Search

- **Titles**: Headlines and content titles
- **Content**: Full text of data points
- **Authors**: When author information is available
- **Outlets**: Filter by specific sources

### Search Operators

| Operator | Example | Result |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Multiple terms | `bitcoin, BTC` | Matches either term |
| Exact phrase | `"bitcoin ETF"` | Matches exact phrase |
| Outlet filter | Select from dropdown | Limits to specific sources |

## Best Practices

### For Comprehensive Coverage

1. **Use multiple variations**: "MicroStrategy, MSTR, Saylor"
2. **Include common misspellings**: If applicable
3. **Add related terms**: Competitors, industry terms

### For Focused Results

1. **Be specific**: "Bitcoin ETF Blackrock" not just "ETF"
2. **Use exact phrases**: For precise matching
3. **Combine with filters**: Date + outlet + sentiment

### For Ongoing Monitoring

1. **Review weekly reports**: Get automated summaries
2. **Set up alerts**: Don't miss important coverage
3. **Check trends**: See the bigger picture
